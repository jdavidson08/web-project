Welcome to Hyde Park Surgery...

We hope to use our surgery website as a way of presenting all our patients with an up to date resource for all information relating to our practice. We will keep it current with any news, developments and details that arerelevant to the practice and our patients.

If you do not speak English and need to view information in a different language, please click on one of the flags on the right hand side of this page. You will then be able to choose which language you would like to view the information in.

As well as a source of information about our Practice, the staff and the services we provide, we hope you use the website as a useful resource with links to other reliable websites for health related information. If there is topical health information we will also add this to the website.

You may book appointments and order prescriptions on-line. You have to call in to the surgery first so that the receptionist can give you a user name and password.
PLEASE DO NOT BOOK APPOINTMENTS ON LINE FOR: COIL or IMPLANT insertion (book an appointment with the Nurse Practitioner to discuss first), INJECTIONS, DRESSINGS AND MINOR SURGERY. THESE APPOINTMENTS ARE USUALLY BOOKED WITH A PRACTICE NURSE.
