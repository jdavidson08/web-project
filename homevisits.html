Home Visits...

Telephone
08443 756070 or 0113 8879551

If you need a home visit, please telephone the surgery BEFORE 10am. If your visit is urgent please specify this and give the receptionist clear, concise details.

The doctor can normally see FOUR patients in the surgery in the time it takes to do ONE home visit.

PLEASE COME TO THE SURGERY IF POSSIBLE.


Test Results...

For test results please telephone the receptionist, on 08443 756070 or 0113 8879551, in the afternoon if possible. The results of tests can only be given to patients after the doctor has seen the result.
